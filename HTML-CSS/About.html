About US

Blessed Streets is a clothing and tech-style company managed by me Aaron J. Hunte, sole trader. We are located Downtown, Port of Spain opposite Excellence Stores. Blessed Streets provides customers with designer, casual and formal clothing. Our products are locally made. Customer service is top-notch, for we have acquired an amazing staff whose only goal is to make customers feel at home. We also encourage customers to take part in various activities; one of our famous games is ‘Jump Shot Thursdays’, where customers who purchase products can test them out in our indoor half-court gym. Both positive and negative feedback is welcomed, as we strive to better ourselves as a business and make our customers happy. We target persons who are looking for top quality products but don’t have the money to purchase them at crazy prices. Our opening hours are from Monday – Thursday from 7:00 am to 4:00 pm. Fridays we are open from 8:00 am to 3:00 pm. And on Sundays from 11:00 am to 2:00 pm.
